Bitboo Safes

BITBOO SAFE is a program where our Associates buy safes and lock coins / tokens, for a certain period of time depending on the safe. By purchasing a safe, they receive a Rewards for which the company allocates 20% of the earnings from its products. After the minimum period of locked coins / tokens in the safe, the coins / tokens are unlocked.
The company offers the possibility for the associate to decide after unlocking the safe whether he wants to sell coins / tokens at the internal exchange or wants to keep them in the safe and receive a dividend from the Rewards. As a long as he keeps coins / tokens in the safe, the associate receives dividend and if he sells his coins / tokens, he loses Rewards and does not receive a dividend for that safe.
An associate can buy an unlimited number of safes with which he increases his Rewards and thus increases his dividend.
